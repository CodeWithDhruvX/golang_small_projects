Welcome to AI Email Response Generator

Transform your Gmail experience with AI-powered email responses. Save time and communicate more effectively with intelligent, context-aware replies.

✨ Key Features

  • AI-powered email response generation
  • Multiple tone options (Professional, Casual, Friendly, Formal)
  • Adjustable response length (Short, Medium, Detailed)
  • Seamless Gmail integration
  • Real-time response generation
  • Secure OAuth2 authentication

🚀 Getting Started

1

Connect Your Gmail Account

Click the extension icon and select "Connect Gmail" to authorize access to your Gmail account.

2

Configure Your Preferences

Set your preferred response tone and length in the extension settings.

3

Start Generating Responses

Open any email in Gmail and click "Generate AI Response" to create a personalized reply.
