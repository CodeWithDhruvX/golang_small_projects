How it works

  1. Click "Connect Gmail" to start the OAuth2 authentication process
  2. Sign in with your Google account and grant the necessary permissions
  3. Once connected, click "Sync Emails" to fetch recent recruiter emails
  4. The system will automatically classify emails and generate AI responses
  5. You can send replies directly through your Gmail account
Security Note: We use OAuth2 authentication and never store your Gmail password. All communication is encrypted and follows Google's security best practices.
